Miswaak Azaan

Never miss a prayer.

A quiet desktop companion that plays the Azaan at the right time — wherever you are.

Download for Windows · v1.0.0 View on GitHub

Free · MIT Licensed · ~79 MB

Heads up: Windows may show a SmartScreen warning because this build is unsigned. Click “More info” → “Run anyway”. It’s safe.

Made for stillness, not noise.

Set it once, then forget about it. Miswaak Azaan stays out of your way until the moment matters.

Global prayer times

Pick your city. Accurate timings powered by Aladhan, with an offline fallback so you’re never caught out.

Automatic Azaan

The Azaan plays at every prayer time without you lifting a finger. Quiet hours and volume are yours to control.

One‑tap test

Hear the Azaan on demand to check your speakers, volume, or just the comfort of the call.

Calm, focused UI

Nothing flashy. Just today’s prayers, the next one ahead, and the time until you hear it.

A glimpse of the app.

Designed to fade into your desktop until the call rises.
